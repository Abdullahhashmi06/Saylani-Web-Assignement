Why we need a mask ?

Wearing masks will help prevent the spread of the infection and prevent the individuals from contracting any airborne infectious fluid. When someone coughs, talks, sneezes they could release germs in form of small, airborne particles, into the air that may infect others nearby. Masks are part of an infection control strategy to eliminate cross-contamination.
